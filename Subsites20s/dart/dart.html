DART

Launch: November 24th, 2022

Mission Duration: 10 Earth Months

Retired: Sep 26th, 2022

Launched on November 24, 2021, NASA's Double Asteroid Redirection Test (DART) was the first-ever planetary defense mission. It successfully collided with asteroid Dimorphos on September 26, 2022, altering its orbit. This groundbreaking mission demonstrated humanity's ability to redirect hazardous asteroids, enhancing Earth's protection from potential impacts.
