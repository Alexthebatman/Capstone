CAPSTONE

Launch: June 28th, 2022

Mission Duration: 2.6 Earth Years (Ongoing)

Retired: N/A

Launched on June 28, 2022, NASA's CAPSTONE (Cislunar Autonomous Positioning System Technology Operations and Navigation Experiment) spacecraft tests a unique lunar orbit for future Artemis missions. Operating in a near-rectilinear halo orbit around the Moon, CAPSTONE validates navigation technologies, paving the way for sustainable human exploration of deep space.
