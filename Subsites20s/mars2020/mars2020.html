MARS 2020

Launch: July 30th, 2020

Mission Duration: 687 Earth days

Retired: N/A

The Mars 2020 mission, launched on July 30, 2020, sent the Perseverance rover and Ingenuity helicopter to explore Mars' Jezero Crater. Perseverance searches for signs of past life and collects samples, while Ingenuity made history with the first powered flight on another planet. The mission continues, advancing exploration and future Mars research.
