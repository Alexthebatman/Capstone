ATLANTIS (STS-135)

Launch: July 8th, 2011

Mission Duration: 12 Days, 18 Hours, 28 Minutes

Retired: July 21st, 2011

Atlantis (STS-135) was the 135th and final mission of NASA's Space Shuttle program. It carried the Raffaello multipurpose logistics module to the International Space Station, delivering supplies and spare parts. A crew of four astronauts completed the mission, which marked the end of a 30-year era of shuttle flights. Atlantis now rests on public display at the Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex.
