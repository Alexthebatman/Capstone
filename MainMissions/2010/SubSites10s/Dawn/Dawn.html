DAWN

Launch: September 27th, 2007

Mission Duration: 11 Years, 1 Month

Retired: November 1st, 2018

The Dawn mission was a groundbreaking NASA project that became the first spacecraft to orbit two extraterrestrial bodiesâ€”Vesta and Ceres. Using ion propulsion, Dawn spent over a year at each body, collecting detailed images and data that transformed our understanding of the early solar system. At Ceres, it discovered evidence of briny water beneath the surface and mysterious bright spots, hinting at geological activity. Dawn operated far beyond expectations until contact ended in 2018.
