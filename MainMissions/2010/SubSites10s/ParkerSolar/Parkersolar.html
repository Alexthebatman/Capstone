PARKER SOLAR PROBE

Launch: August 12th, 2018

Mission Duration: Ongoing

Retired: N/A

The Parker Solar Probe is NASA’s daring mission to "touch the Sun." Designed to travel closer to the Sun than any previous spacecraft, it investigates the Sun’s outer atmosphere (corona) to help solve fundamental questions about solar wind and space weather. The probe uses a revolutionary heat shield to survive extreme temperatures and is providing unprecedented data from within the Sun's corona.
