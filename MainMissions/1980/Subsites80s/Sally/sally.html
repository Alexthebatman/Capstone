SALLY'S FLIGHT (STS-7)

Launch: June 18th, 1983

Mission Duration: 6 Days, 2 Hours, 23 Minutes

Retired: June 24th, 1983

STS-7 was a historic NASA Space Shuttle mission that launched aboard Challenger and made astronaut Sally Ride the first American woman in space. The mission deployed two communication satellites and conducted several science experiments using the Shuttle's payload bay.
