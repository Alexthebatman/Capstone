PHOENIX LANDER

Launch: August 4th, 2007

Mission Duration: 5 Months

Retired: November 10th, 2008

The Phoenix Mars Lander was designed to study the history of water and potential habitability in the Martian arctic. It confirmed the presence of water ice below the Martian surface and conducted extensive soil analysis. Although not intended to survive a Martian winter, Phoenix returned valuable data until solar energy ran out in November 2008.
