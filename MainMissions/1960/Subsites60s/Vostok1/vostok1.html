VOSTOK 1

Launch: April 12th, 1961

Mission Duration: 1 Hour & 48 Minutes

Retired: June 1963

On April 12, 1961, the Soviet Union launched Vostok 1, carrying Yuri Gagarin, who became the first human to journey into outer space and orbit the Earth. This mission marked a significant milestone in human space exploration and intensified the space race during the Cold War era.
