GEMINI 4

Launch: June 3rd, 1965

Mission Duration: 4 Days, 1 Hour, 56 Minutes, & 12 Seconds

Retired: November 15th, 1966

On June 3, 1965, Gemini 4 launched with astronauts James McDivitt and Ed White. During this mission, Ed White performed the first American spacewalk, demonstrating the feasibility of extravehicular activity and advancing the United States' capabilities in human spaceflight.
