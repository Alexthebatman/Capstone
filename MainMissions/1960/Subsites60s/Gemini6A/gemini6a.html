GEMINI 6A

Launch: December 15th, 1965

Mission Duration: 1 Day, 1 Hour, 51 Minutes, & 54 Seconds

Retired: December 16th, 1965

Launched on December 15, 1965, Gemini 6A carried Walter Schirra and Thomas Stafford into orbit for the first successful crewed space rendezvous with Gemini 7. The mission demonstrated precision orbital maneuvering and paved the way for docking operations essential to the Apollo program.
