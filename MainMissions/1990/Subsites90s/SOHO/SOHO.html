SOLAR AND HELIOSPHERIC OBSERVATORY (SOHO)

Launch: December 2nd, 1995

Mission Duration: 29+ Years (Ongoing)

Retired: N/A

The Solar and Heliospheric Observatory (SOHO) is a joint mission by ESA and NASA dedicated to studying the Sun. Launched in 1995, SOHO has helped scientists monitor solar activity, predict space weather, and discover over 4,000 comets through its coronagraph observations.
