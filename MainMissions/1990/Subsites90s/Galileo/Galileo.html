GALILEO

Launch: October 18th, 1989

Mission Duration: 14 Years

Retired: September 21st, 2003

Galileo was the first spacecraft to orbit Jupiter and deployed a probe into its atmosphere. During its 14-year mission, Galileo revolutionized our understanding of the gas giant and its moons, including evidence of subsurface oceans on Europa. It also studied Jupiter's magnetosphere and delivered close-up images of many Jovian satellites before being intentionally deorbited into Jupiter.
