COMPTON GAMMA RAY OBSERVATORY

Launch: April 5th, 1991

Mission Duration: 9 Years

Retired: June 4th, 2000

The Compton Gamma Ray Observatory was launched aboard Space Shuttle Atlantis and was the second of NASA's Great Observatories. It studied gamma-ray bursts, pulsars, quasars, and black holes, revolutionizing our understanding of high-energy astrophysics before being deorbited in 2000 after a successful mission.
